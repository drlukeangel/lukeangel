Did you know you ca become[![El Camino Fire Academy Logo](https://i2.wp.com/www.elcamino.edu/fire_academy/images/ECFA.png?resize=101%2C100)](http://www.elcamino.edu/fire_academy/) a volunteer fire fighter and work at most public fire stations? Its not a easy task but if your up for it its fun and rewarding. I went to the El Camino Fire Academy to get my certifications and then on to volunteer at several different stations.

**

The Academy’s core content has been consistent in meeting the highest level of firefighter training. The requirements for physical fitness for each recruit are both strenuous and demanding and only exceeded by the academic standards where excellence is expected, not just requested.

**

 

If you not up to doing that you can also help your city by [Becoming Cert Certified.](http://lukeangel.co/give/help-your-community-out-by-becoming-cert-certified/)
