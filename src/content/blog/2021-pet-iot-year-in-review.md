## What got added in 2021

- **AirTag** (April) — testing concluded; not used for Atom (anti-stalking chirp issue).
- **Whistle Health & GPS Plus** (August) — Atom now wears two collars.
- **A second SureFlap door** (back deck, May) — adds redundancy.
- **An iRobot Roomba i7+** (December, technically smart-home) — relevant to pet IoT because it has to navigate around Joule + Boson.

## What worked

- **The Whistle Health vitals data**. Six months of baseline now established. No anomalies yet but the trend tracking is real.
- **Fi on Atom**. Battery life continues to track ~14 days. Reliable.
- **SureFlap Hub data for two cats**. Per-cat outdoor patterns informative.

## What didn't

- **AirTag for pet tracking**. Architectural mismatch between anti-stalking and intentional pet tracking. Not the right tool.
- **Halo Collar reviews**. Welfare concerns confirmed by independent reviews. Not buying.
- **Litter-Robot per-cat attribution**. Still no native feature; still relying on my CSV-export script.
