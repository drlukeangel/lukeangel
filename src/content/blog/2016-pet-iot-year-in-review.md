End of 2016. Big year.

## Scoring the 2015 forecast

| Prediction | Confidence | Outcome | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| Whistle 3 with built-in cellular | 85% | Shipped April | ✓ |
| Whistle acquired by pet conglomerate | 65% | Mars Petcare, April | ✓ |
| Furbo Kickstarter ships in 2016 | 50% | Pushed to 2017 | ✗ |
| SureFlap Hub ships | 80% | Announced, slipped to 2017 | ✗ |
| Petnet first major outage | 60% | Minor outages, no major event yet | ✓ (partial) |
| Fitness for cats attempt | 40% | Pawbo released a "cat fitness" thing; it's terrible. | ✓ (technically) |
| First HomeKit pet device | 35% | Nothing | ✗ |
| Tagg shuts down or pivots | 55% | Still hanging on; rumored Mars-Tagg discussions | ✗ |

5/8 hits + 1 partial = roughly **65%**. Worse than 2015's 75%. The misses were all "vendor ships when vendor ships" timing — Furbo, SureFlap Hub, HomeKit pet device. I keep predicting on calendars; vendors don't.

## What got added in 2016

- **Whistle 3** (May, $99 + $6.95/mo). Shelved by October after the [six-month review](/blog/six-months-on-whistle-3-cellular-realities/).
- **Petnet SmartFeeder** (full year of use after November 2015 ship). Mostly working; occasional missed feeds.
- Nothing for Joule. The cat-IoT gap remains.

## What worked

- **Whistle 2 + FitBark on Atom**. Reliable, dual-redundant, no subscription. The pre-cellular era's best combo.
- **SureFlap door** for Joule (year and a half in now). Continued 100% read reliability. The neighbor's gray tabby has stopped trying.
- **Petnet SmartFeeder for vacations**. Schedule + portion control + remote-feed via the app. Worked for two summer vacations.

## What didn't

- **Whistle 3's subscription model**. The hardware bricks without the $6.95/month. I'm not interested in pet hardware that becomes a paperweight when I stop paying.
- **The Mars-owned Whistle recommendations layer**. Marketing-as-care-advice. Disabled where I can.
- **The cat-IoT gap**. Nothing new for cats in 2016 except announcement of the SureFlap Hub. Joule continues to be under-served by the industry.

## What I'm buying in 2017

- SureFlap Hub when it ships.
- Furbo if/when it ships.
- Litter-Robot III Connect when the WiFi version arrives.
- Probably skipping Fi v1 — wait for v2 to mature.

## What's next

The Petnet long-term review coming in Q1 2017. Then Furbo and the smart-camera category when devices land. Maybe SureFlap Hub finally too.

Three years of pet IoT documented. Dog side maturing. Cat side still thin. Mars consolidation accelerating. Same patterns as every other IoT category: vendor cloud dominance, subscription creep, conflict-of-interest data ownership.
