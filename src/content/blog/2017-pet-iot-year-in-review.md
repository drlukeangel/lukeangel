End of 2017. Five years of pet IoT documented.

## Scoring the 2016 forecast

| Prediction | Confidence | Outcome | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| SureFlap Hub ships | 90% | Yes, March | ✓ |
| Furbo ships consumer hardware | 75% | Yes, March | ✓ |
| Fi smart collar announces Series A | 60% | Yes, October — $5M seed | ✓ |
| Petnet first multi-day outage | 55% | Two single-day outages, no multi-day yet | ✗ (partial credit; trend is there) |
| Litter-Robot WiFi version | 80% | Announced, ships 2018 | ✗ (timing) |
| Mars acquires another pet-tech startup | 65% | Mars invested in IAMS pet-tracking pilot but no acquisition | ✗ (close) |
| First HomeKit pet product | 30% | Nothing | ✗ |
| Tagg shuts down | 60% | Tagg Acquired by Whistle (Mars) in November; product is being phased out | ✓ (partial; "shut down" is happening over 2018) |

4/8 hits + 2 partials = roughly **63%**. Repeating the timing-misses pattern.

## What got added in 2017

- **Furbo Dog Camera** (April) — running fine, treat-toss daily.
- **SureFlap Hub** (May) — Joule's door data finally networked.
- **A backup PetSafe Smart Feed feeder** (June) — non-connected, mechanical-timer-only, installed as Petnet failover for Joule. Petnet hasn't failed catastrophically yet, but the redundancy is there.

## What worked

- **The cat-IoT story improved meaningfully**. SureFlap Hub + the connected door = real data on Joule's outdoor patterns.
- **Furbo treat-tossing** for Atom's "good dog" rewards while I'm at work.
- **The Whistle 2 still chugging along** — battery degraded but functional.

## What didn't

- **Furbo's bark detection**. Disabled it after a week.
- **Petnet's reliability creeping worse**. Two single-day outages in 2017; cloud feels increasingly fragile.
- **No HomeKit pet device** — third year predicting it, third year wrong.

## What I'm buying in 2018

- Litter-Robot III Connect when it ships.
- Fi if reviews are credible.
- A SureFlap microchip feeder if/when it lands.
- Definitely not another Petnet (the backup PetSafe stays installed).

## What's next

Big year coming. Litter-Robot Connect will be the first smart-litter device in the house. Fi will (probably) ship and finally give Atom a non-Mars-owned alternative. The Petnet question — does it survive another year? — looms.
