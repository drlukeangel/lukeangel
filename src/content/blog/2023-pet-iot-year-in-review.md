4/8 hits + 1 partial = ~56%. Worse than recent years.

## What got added this year

- **Move into new house** (October). Pet-IoT layout redesigned around 42 Cat6 drops, planned conduit, multi-cat litter closet.
- **Litter-Robot 4** (one unit; second still on order). The 4 is meaningfully better than 3 — quieter, better weight sensing.
- **A second SureFlap door** (back door on the new house — the front patio still uses the original).
- **A vet visit for Atom** flagged by his resting HR drift — turned out to be early-stage mitral valve issue (common in senior Labs). Lifestyle adjustments + monitoring; no medication yet.

## What worked

- **Whistle Health vitals catching Atom's HR drift** before it became obvious. The 8% elevation showed up in early summer; vet appointment in September confirmed mitral murmur.
- **New house's pet infrastructure**. The litter closet (with both Litter-Robots, ventilated) is a huge quality-of-life improvement.
- **Petivity per-cat attribution** stays at ~87% accurate.

## What didn't

- **Litter-Robot 4 cycle frequency**. The new unit is slightly more aggressive about cycles than the III; the cats are confused.
- **The "AI behavior detection" pitch**. None of it credible. Disabled all such features.
