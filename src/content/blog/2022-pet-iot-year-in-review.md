6/8 hits + 1 partial = **75%**. Decent year.

## What got added in 2022

- **Quark** (April) — second dog, Fi collar from day one.
- **Halo Collar** (June, returned July).
- **Petivity smart litter monitor** (July).
- **Litter-Robot 4** (September) — replaced the basement Litter-Robot III.

## What worked

- **Petivity's multi-cat ML attribution** (87% agreement with my own script).
- **Quark's Fi onboarding**. Smooth.
- **Atom's Whistle Health vitals**. 16 months of baseline. Still no anomalies, but baseline is rock-solid for future detection.

## What didn't

- **Halo Collar**. Engineering excellence applied to wrong problem.
- **The "AI behavior detection" promises** from Furbo + others. Nothing credible shipped.
- **Multi-Litter-Robot integration**. Two Litter-Robots in the house but no unified per-cat dashboard.

## What I'm buying in 2023

- A new house full of pet infrastructure planning (Cat6 to the litter-box closet, microchip-feeder for the kitchen, second SureFlap door for the new layout).

- Whatever Mars-independent vitals tracker emerges, if any.

## What's next

The new build move-in is the big story for 2023. Four pets relocating to a new house with intentionally-designed pet IoT infrastructure. The first time I've gotten to plan it from the wires up.
