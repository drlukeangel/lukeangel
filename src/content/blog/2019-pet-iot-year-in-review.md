End of 2019. Seven years of pet IoT documented.

## Scoring the 2018 forecast

| Prediction | Confidence | Outcome | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fi ships consumer device | 80% | Yes, October-November | ✓ |
| Petnet first multi-day outage | 75% | Several long single-day outages, no multi-day catastrophe yet | ✗ (close) |
| Whistle Switch announced | 60% | Whistle Health & GPS Plus rumored, no formal vitals announcement yet | ✗ |
| Smart-litter competitor launches | 60% | A few cheap competitors (Catlink, Petree); none credible threat to Litter-Robot | ✓ (partial) |
| Mars Petcare vet-clinic strategy visible | 70% | AniCura integration with Banfield; visible | ✓ |
| First credible HomeKit pet device | 35% | Nothing | ✗ |
| First Halo-Collar-style GPS-fence device | 50% | Halo Collar founded, hasn't shipped yet | ✗ (delayed) |
| Buy Fi for Atom, decommission Whistle | 70% | Yes | ✓ |

4/8 hits + 1 partial = 50%. Roughly average for me. The misses were timing (Halo, Whistle Switch, Petnet's catastrophe).

## What got added in 2019

- **Fi Series 1** for Atom (November).
- **Backup PetSafe Smart Feed** is now my primary feeder; Petnet remains as secondary.

## What worked

- **Fi's 14-day battery in real conditions**. The big engineering win of the year.
- **Litter-Robot III Connect's per-visit data**. Still no anomalies in Joule's patterns; baseline is now established.

## What didn't

- **Petnet's slow decline**. Outages got more frequent + longer. Communication from the company is essentially zero.
- **The HomeKit pet device gap** — three years of predicting it, three years of nothing.

## What I'm buying in 2020

- Whatever PetSafe Smart Feed v2 looks like (when shipping).
- Maybe a backup Litter-Robot if we add a second cat.
- Probably not the rumored Halo Collar (ethical concerns).

## What's next

The Petnet endgame, finally, this year. The COVID-era pet adoption boom is something I'm starting to see in 2019 data (more friends getting dogs); 2020 is going to surge it harder. Possibly a new pet in our house too — we've been talking about a second cat for a while.

Seven years in. The Mars consolidation has shaped the category in ways nobody saw in 2013. The cloud-dependency lesson is about to be written in capital letters when Petnet finally goes.
